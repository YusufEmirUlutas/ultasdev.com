Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Uza?
After downloading the app, complete the onboarding process where you'll enter your measurements and goals. The app will create a personalized workout plan based on your profile.
How often should I do the exercises?
For best results, follow your daily workout plan which typically includes morning and evening sessions. Consistency is key to seeing improvements in posture and flexibility.
Can I change my workout schedule?
Yes! You can adjust your workout times and preferences in the Settings section of the app.
Is Uza suitable for all ages?
Uza is designed for users aged 13 and above. However, we recommend consulting with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise program, especially for users under 18 or those with existing health conditions.
How do I track my progress?
The app includes a progress tracking feature where you can log your measurements and view your improvement over time through charts and analytics.
What if I experience discomfort during exercises?
Stop immediately if you feel pain or unusual discomfort. The exercises should create a stretching sensation but never pain. Consult with a healthcare professional if discomfort persists.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage your subscription through your device's App Store settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions, find Uza, and select Cancel Subscription.
Will I definitely grow taller?
Results vary based on individual factors including age, genetics, and consistency. Uza focuses on optimizing posture and spinal health, which can help you reach your maximum potential height.
